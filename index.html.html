The Process

Up and running
in minutes.

NotGym installs once and runs forever. No ongoing internet. No accounts. No data collection. Your kids stay safe, your data stays home.

01
Download & Install

Run the one-click installer. AI models, voice engine, and pose detection are all bundled and ready in under 3 minutes — no technical knowledge needed.

02
Pick Your Exercise

Open NotGym, point your webcam, and choose from 5 exercises. Animated form guides show you exactly how to position yourself before you begin.

03

The AI tracks your pose, counts reps, checks form, and gives spoken feedback — all at 30fps with zero server round trips. Just like a personal trainer.
